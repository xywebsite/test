Introduction to Raster and Vector

Today, I learnt about the basics of what Raster and Vecctor is.

I learnt Raster deals with pixels on the screen and is used for photographs, images.
Formats: .bmp,.jpg,.pn

Vector uses mathematical functions and is characterized by clean lines.
Formats: .dxf, .svg, .STEP

I was tasked to remove background from a selfie, and substitute it with a seaside scene.

1. Find a photo of yourself.

2. Remove the background with Magic Wand Tool and Eraser to refine the edges, and resize the photo to 1024x1024 in Photoshop.

3. Add a seaside scene and export!

I was tasked to choose an image of an animal and trace it to form a vector art.

1. Find a photo of an animal. I chose a cat.

2. Trace the image of the cat after cropping it nicely. I used Image Trace.

3.Use Image Trace on the cropped image. The shadows and outlines will show up.

4. Trace the face of the cat and add a solid colour.

5. With reference from the shadows, create fur and outlines of its features.
