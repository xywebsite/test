Introduction to Laser Cutting.

Also known as CNC (Computer Numerical Control).

CAD provides the necessary data from design files, controls cutting & marking of material. It is very precise and repeatable

Laser Cutting a Name Tag.

I used Fusion 360 to sketch the nametag and cut the words out.

I then exported it into a DXF file.

I then imported the file into Corel Draw.

Then, I sent the file to the laser machine to cut it out.

I then exported it into a DXF file.
